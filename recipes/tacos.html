Ingredients

  • 10 Taco shells
  • 1 bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 lb Ground beef
  • 1 seasoning packet
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 package of shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef and add seasoning packet
  2. Dice your tomatoe
  3. Steam your taco shells if using soft tortillas
  4. Add ground beef to shell
  5. Add lettuce
  6. Add tomatoes
  7. Add sauces
  8. Add sour cream
  9. Enjoy

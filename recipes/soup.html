Ingredients

  • 6 cups of chicken broth
  • 1lb ground Italian sausage
  • 1lb diced red potatoes
  • 1 cup fresh kale
  • 1 diced yellow onion
  • 1 table spoon of garlic
  • 1/2 cupp heavy whipping cream
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook sausage till cokked through
  2. Add onion and sautee till translucent
  3. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds
  4. Add potatoes and mix thoroughly
  5. Add broth and let simmer for 30 min
  6. Add heavy whipping cream
  7. Add kale and simmer another 5 min
